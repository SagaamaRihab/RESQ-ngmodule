Emergency Instructions

Carefully follow these instructions in case of emergency.

Stay Calm

Avoid panic and follow the instructions provided by the RESQ system.

Follow the Routes

Use only marked exits. Do not use elevators.

Help People in Need

Assist people with reduced mobility.

Reach the Meeting Point

Move away from the building and wait for official instructions.

⚠️ In case of immediate danger, always follow the authorities' instructions.
